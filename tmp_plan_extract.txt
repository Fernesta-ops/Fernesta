1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Grassfield Marketing is a female-led, full-service marketing agency built for Indian SMEs and D2C brands.
Founded and led by Ojasvini, Grassfield operates on a scalable, systems-driven delivery model — combining
brand strategy, performance marketing, content, SEO, and analytics under one accountable roof. The agency is
anchored in a clear philosophy: strategy before execution, data before opinion, story before sale.
India's 63 million SMEs are chronically underserved by marketing. Large agencies ignore them. Freelancers let
them down. Grassfield fills this gap — with consistent, professional, outcomes-oriented delivery at price points
built for the real Indian market.

2. MISSION & VISION
Mission
“To be the most trusted marketing partner for Indian SMEs and D2C brands — delivering
consistent, data-led, accountable marketing that creates real business outcomes, not just
activity.”
Grassfield exists to solve a specific and persistent problem: the majority of Indian businesses that need
professional marketing cannot access it reliably. Large agencies are priced out of their reach. Freelancers are
inconsistent and unaccountable. In-house teams are expensive and slow to build. Grassfield closes this gap —
with structured delivery, clear accountability, and pricing built for the real Indian business landscape.
Vision
“To build India's leading scalable marketing platform for the underserved middle market —
a female-founded institution that proves disciplined, process-driven marketing at scale is
not the exclusive domain of large enterprises.”
By Year 3, Grassfield will operate as a platform — not just a team. A core leadership layer supported by a
curated network of trained specialists, all delivering within Grassfield's proprietary frameworks and quality
standards. Growth will be driven by systems, not headcount.

4. MARKET OPPORTUNITY & COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
The Market Problem
India has over 63 million MSMEs and a rapidly growing D2C ecosystem projected to reach $60 billion by 2027.
The vast majority of these businesses need professional marketing to grow — yet their current options are
deeply inadequate:
  Large agencies: Minimum retainers of Rs. 3–5L/month, focused on enterprise clients, uninterested in SMEs
  Mid-size agencies: Inconsistent quality, high staff turnover, over-promise and under-deliver, often
misaligned on goals
  Freelancers: No accountability, no strategic layer, unreliable timelines, zero continuity across channels
  In-house teams: Expensive to build, slow to hire, hard to maintain skill breadth across SEO, SEM, content,
brand simultaneously

Year 1 is about proving the model, not scaling it. The goal is to establish Grassfield as a credible, operational
marketing agency with a growing retainer base, a documented delivery system, and a founder who is visibly
leading the business. Every decision in Year 1 is made in service of this foundation.
“Ojasvini runs all operations personally from Day 1. No interns, no hires until the business
demands it. The lean start is not a constraint — it is the strategy.”

8. TALENT PLAN & HIRING ROADMAP
Philosophy: Hire for Need, Train for Culture
Grassfield does not hire ahead of revenue. Every addition to the team is triggered by a specific, demonstrated
business need. The agency follows a two-track hiring model: freshers trained inhouse by Amit to build a
pipeline of Grassfield-native talent, and experienced professionals hired selectively where client complexity
demands specialist depth. Both tracks operate within Grassfield frameworks delivering consistency regardless
of seniority.
“Amit plays a central role as training lead for all freshers — building a talent pipeline that is
cost-efficient, culturally aligned, and operationally ready from Day 1 of deployment.”
Women-First Hiring Policy
Grassfield Marketing is committed to building a women-majority workforce. This is not a compliance posture
— it is a deliberate organisational philosophy. Women bring underrepresented perspectives to marketing
strategy; a women-majority agency serving women-led and women-targeting brands delivers more authentic
work; and building visible female leadership at every level strengthens Grassfield credibility as a female-founded
institution.
  Minimum 60% of all hires across all levels will be women
  Preference given to women candidates at equal qualification across fresher, junior, and specialist tracks
  Ojasvini leads all hiring decisions ensuring the policy is upheld with founder-level accountability
  Women-majority workforce strengthens eligibility under WEP, iStart Rajasthan, CII/FICCI women
programs, and CSR-linked hiring grants
  Career progression paths designed to retain women through flexible, remote-first work structure
Grant Note: A documented women-majority hiring policy with verifiable workforce data significantly strengthens
grant applications under WEP Ignite, iStart Rajasthan, and any scheme that evaluates women empowerment as
a selection criterion. This is a genuine competitive advantage in the grant landscape.
